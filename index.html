FAQs

Do you need water and electricity access?
Yes, we require access to water and electricity at your location for most detailing services.
Where do you serve?
We currently operate in selected areas of Delhi, Noida, Ghaziabad, and Gurgaon.
Do you bring your own equipment?
Yes, we carry all necessary tools, products, and equipment to your doorstep.
How long does a full detail take?
A full detail can take 3 to 6 hours depending on the car size and condition.
How do I book a service?
You can book via WhatsApp through the provided buttons or by messaging us directly.
Do you use safe products?
Yes, we only use professional-grade, pH-balanced products safe for your car.
What is paint correction?
Paint correction removes swirls, scratches, and oxidation to restore a glossy finish.
Can you clean leather interiors?
Yes, we deep clean and condition leather seats using specialized leather care products.
Do you clean engine bays?
Yes, upon request. We use low-pressure, water-safe techniques for engine bay cleaning.
What are your operating hours?
We work from 8 AM to 7 PM every day, including weekends.
